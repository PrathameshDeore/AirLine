About

FlightBooking.com EASY TO BOOK TICKETS A flight booking system project that allows users to search, book, and manage flights. It typically includes functionalities such as checking flight availability, selecting departure and arrival times, choosing seats, making payments, and generating tickets. The system integrates with multiple airlines Booking systems and provides users with real-time information about flight schedules, prices, and routes. The goal of a flight booking system is to make the process of booking and managing flights more convenient and efficient for users
